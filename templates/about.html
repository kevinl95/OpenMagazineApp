The Colorado Engineer Magazine is a student run publication. Since its creation in 1904, it has consistently explored the latest and greatest in the world of engineering, science and technology. Thank you for supporting this legacy.

Student Opportunities

The Colorado Engineer staff is comprised completely of volunteer students from the University of Colorado at Boulder. If you are a CU student and you are interested in writing, photographing, or editing for the magazine, we would love to hear from you! Please fill out the student employment interest form, and our staff recruiter will be in contact with you shortly.
